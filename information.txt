📍 Overview

Livable Hotel Bangkok is a modern boutique hotel located in the Ratchathewi / Victory Monument area of Bangkok. It is known for its clean minimalist design, peaceful atmosphere, and convenient access to public transportation and shopping districts.

📌 Location
Address: 89/9 Rang Nam Alley, Phaya Thai, Ratchathewi, Bangkok, Thailand

Near shopping areas such as Pratunam and CentralWorld
Adjacent to Santiphap Park, providing a quieter and greener environment than typical city hotels
