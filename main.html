Hotel Facilities

Rooftop
Pool

Our garden-level pool is a peaceful spot to unwind after a day exploring Bangkok. Open to all hotel guests — a tranquil backdrop for photos or a quiet afternoon dip.

Surroundings

Park View Rooms

Several of our rooms overlook a tree-lined garden — quiet, green, and a genuine relief from the city's noise. Guests frequently mention it as an unexpected highlight.

Getting Around

Airport Rail Link

Ratchaprarop Station is about 10 minutes on foot — a direct connection to Suvarnabhumi Airport. No need to book a taxi for your arrival or departure.

Guest Services

Self-Service Laundry

Coin-operated washers and dryers available on-site for a small fee. Especially useful for longer stays — no need to pack as much or rely on pricey laundry services.

Dining

Doux
Café

Our in-house café, Doux, is open daily for hotel guests and visitors. We serve a proper breakfast, a rotating selection of à la carte dishes, and a full drinks menu — from morning coffee to evening cocktails. Nothing overly complicated, just good food in a comfortable setting.
